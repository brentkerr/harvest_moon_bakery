About

Cooking meals from scratch and working to perfect family recipes is something my family has done for as long as I can remember. We haved always enjoyed taking the time to create delicious food, but for me there was always something special about baking bread. Bread is one of the most ancient foods and somehow connects us to our heritage.

After about three years of working to perfect the sourdough bread, my wife and I finally have something we are proud of. We now offer a handful of breads and are excited about adding more variety. We look forward to sharing our products with you and your families.

Why is sourdough better than regular bread? Real sourdough is fermented. We make our sourdough with our own starter which has its own naturally occurring yeast. When you eat bread made with this process, some of the gluten is broken down, making it lower on the glycemic index and easier to digest.
